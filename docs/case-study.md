# Case Study

## Problem

A cafe with strong visual material needs more than a static page. The website should help visitors feel the brand, understand the menu direction, trust the visit, and move toward enquiry or arrival.

## Approach

I shaped the prototype direction around a scroll-led visitor journey:

- media-led first impression
- signature drinks and menu categories
- atmosphere and product storytelling
- location and visit confidence
- simple enquiry direction

## Key Decisions

- Use motion to reveal the brand story progressively.
- Keep the layout focused on appetite and visit intent.
- Separate public presentation from private QA/profile material.
- Treat media-heavy source folders carefully before publishing.
- Avoid making the repo a free copy of the implementation.

## Outcome

The case study demonstrates practical hospitality UX and media-aware frontend thinking in a way that recruiters can scan quickly.
